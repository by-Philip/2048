FAQ

How to play 2048?
Use your ARROWS or WASD to move the tiles.
When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge it into one.
Is this the official website of 2048?
Yes, this is the original website of 2048, unfortunately there are other websites that have 'stolen' our concept and/or changed the identity of the developers of this game.

Is there a 2048 mobile application?
No, unfortunately there isn't a mobile application of 2048.

Why?

We were busy publishing a mobile app for Android and IOS. Unfortunately, other companies have stolen our concept and made an application themselves. As a result, we did not continue with the development.
Why is 2048 in English and not in Dutch?
This is a Dutch website. 2048 was developed and released in English a few years ago. We have deliberately chosen to make this known internationally and not only in the Netherlands. In the end we succeeded, other companies / developers copied our idea and after a while we got our website online. Now we have decided to republish 2048, but as 'math game / challenge / strategic game'. Also this time in English because we hope we can have a success again with this game. In the near future we want to add Dutch and other languages to the game.
